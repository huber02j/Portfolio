About


I am a hardworking, passionate, and motivated software developer that yearns to make a significant impact in this world. My interest in computer programming is trumped only by my desire to improve the lives of people often forgotten or overlooked. This has led me to study Computer Science and Theology and participate in social concern seminars at the University of Notre Dame with the hope of pursuing a career in software development.

"You cannot carry out fundamental change without a certain amount of madness. In this case, it comes from non-conformity; the ability to turn your back on old formulas; the courage to invent the future. It took the madmen of yesterday for us to be able to act with extreme clarity today. I want to be one of those madmen. We must dare to invent the future."

Thomas Sankura

I want to be one of those madmen. Last summer I interned at Epic where I created a full stack web application using Typescript and C#. While I learned valuable software development experience, I also learned the importance in ensuring the programs I create actually address a need in our society. I have no desire to work on something that is successful but does not improve the life of another person.

The summer before I worked with BOSCO-Uganda – a non-profit NGO in Northern Uganda – in their effort to connect people while preserving culture. While there I led a team of Ugandan interns in creating the BOSCO-DMICU(data monitoring interface for computer users) that was implemented within a refugee settlement and secondary school computer centers. This was developed using Python, Windows PowerShell Process commands, C# Windows Forms, and Microsoft SQL.

When I am not busy working with computers, I enjoy playing soccer for Notre Dame’s Varsity Men’s soccer team as a rostered walk-on. With the time left I find myself drawn to learning about life’s deeper questions through a Theology minor and investigating intricate international and domestic social issues through seminars with the Center for Social Concerns.

If you are looking for someone who only stops when the job is done, always finds a way, is willing to risk stepping outside of the comfort zone, and hungry to tackle big problems, then I am the one for you.
